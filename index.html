About ADAPT Academy

Development-First Club Basketball

In a youth basketball world focused on wins, rankings, and exposure, ADAPT focuses on what actually lasts: real development, confidence, and love for the game. Based out of DETAIL Miami in Miami Lakes, ADAPT Academy is South Florida's most innovative AAU program, blending European-style basketball development with deep character building and a true family environment.

European Development Model

Our leadership has trained and studied basketball across 32 countries and 12 European basketball cultures. We bring the best of international development: more practices than games, stable rosters, teaching players to read the game instead of memorizing plays, and building team-first basketball IQ through live situations rather than robotic drills.

Development Over Winning

We don't chase wins, rankings, or exposure. We build environments where progress is unavoidable. Our athletes learn how to think the game, make quicker and smarter decisions, handle pressure, compete with purpose, and ultimately enjoy basketball again. When development comes first, everything else follows.

Everything Under One Roof

ADAPT operates as a full HQ for basketball and human development. Training, psychological development, film sessions, performance training, recovery, academic support, mentorship, all available at our home facility. No driving between multiple locations. One stop, all the resources your athlete needs.

Small Rosters, Real Attention

Each team has a maximum of 9 to 10 players. That means real playing time, genuine coach relationships, and true development, not sitting on a bench hoping to get minutes. Every athlete gets seen, heard, and developed intentionally.

True Family Culture

This isn't a tagline, it's how we operate. Athletes who came through the program years ago still stay connected and get love from the staff. We genuinely care about each individual and build lifelong relationships that extend far beyond the court.

Individualized Development

Custom player development plans tailored to each athlete's specific needs. Film sessions, one-on-one skill work, and continuous feedback so every athlete has a clear path to improvement regardless of where they start.

This program isn't part-time. Athletes get team practices, skills trainings, performance sessions, film, open gym, and tournaments, six days a week. The kind of consistent, structured environment that creates real transformation.

We understand that transportation can be a barrier for underserved families. Our staff actively works to help coordinate carpooling among families to make sure athletes can get to the facility consistently.

Culture & Family

More Than a Team, a Family

Before anything else, our mission is to build better humans and foster a close family environment for the athletes we serve. At ADAPT, we don't just coach players for a season and move on. We invest in them as people.

Athletes who came through our programs years ago still get love from the staff, still come back to train, and still feel like they belong. That's what family means to us. When a scholarship athlete joins ADAPT, they're not a charity case, they're part of the family. They'll be treated, supported, and developed just like every other athlete in the program.

Our coaches build genuine relationships. They know what's going on in their athletes' lives, academically, personally, and on the court. That level of care is what makes the difference for young people who need it most.

After-School Access

Athletes can arrive right after school to do homework, train, hang out, and be in a positive environment until practices and team activities begin. A second home.

Lifelong Relationships

We still show love to athletes from years ago. Once you're part of ADAPT, you're part of the family, and that doesn't have an expiration date.

Holistic Support

Academic monitoring, mental health resources, life skills seminars, and personal check-ins. We see the full picture of every young person we serve.

Bonus Opportunity

Summer Academy, An Added Experience

In addition to the year-round ADAPT program, scholarship athletes could also attend our renowned Summer Academy at DETAIL Miami for select weeks during the summer. For three consecutive summers, athletes from around the world have traveled to Miami for this life-changing training experience.

The Summer Academy offers daily on-court sessions, performance training, competitive open runs and tournaments, recovery, unique Miami experiences, and exposure to a truly global basketball community with athletes from multiple countries and US states training side by side.

This could serve as an incredible supplemental experience for scholarship athletes during school breaks, keeping them active, developing, and connected to the basketball family year-round.

Summer Academy Includes:

  • Daily on-court skills and team training sessions
  • Performance training and supplemental workouts
  • Filmed open runs and tournament-style games
  • Recovery specialist access
  • Miami experiences and sightseeing 1-2x per week
  • Professional content and media features
  • College and pro athlete mentorship
  • Free Virtual Academy program post-summer
  • Athletes from 30+ countries and all US regions
